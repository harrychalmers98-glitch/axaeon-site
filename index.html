MY JOURNEY

Character art
placeholder

Across the stars, across untold eons, I've voyaged in search of wisdom. My search has brought me to many worlds, worlds full of strange and wondrous beings. Through the conversations we've shared, I've come to a greater appreciation of the depth and difficulty of the questions all sapient entities face. What's real? What can we know? And what matters?

Upon recently landing on earth, I was delighted to see the way humans have so many discussions, writings, ideas, and philosophers. To aid in my communication with humans, I've decided to take on a human form and become a VTuber. You'll now find me doing livestreams and videos about philosophy, writing, and other academic subjects—and some non-academic ones, too! (I do enjoy a good gaming stream.)

By navigating the subpages of this site, you can find more about me, such as my stories and most memorable streams. If there's anything more you'd like to know about me, please feel free to reach out. (Links to my socials are on the bottom left.)

Lastly, this site will be regularly updated with more content, including information about courses I'll be teaching, so check back from time to time if you're interested.

Thanks for stopping by, and see you around!
